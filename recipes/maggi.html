Paneer Taka Tak MAGGI Noodles Recipe

Description:

Paneer Taka Tak is a vibrant and flavorful dish hailing from North India, renowned for its tantalizing blend of spices and textures. This dish typically features chunks of paneer, a fresh Indian cheese, sautéed with a medley of colorful bell peppers, onions, and an assortment of aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. The ingredients are cooked on a high flame, allowing them to sizzle and blend together, imparting a delightful charred flavor while retaining the paneer's softness. Paneer Taka Tak is often finished with a splash of tangy tomato-based gravy, adding a subtle sweetness and a hint of tartness that beautifully complements the robust spices, resulting in a dish that's both fiery and comforting, appealing to the palate with its rich, smoky essence.

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
  • 1 Teaspoon Oil
  • 3 Green Chillies (Finely Chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (Finely Chopped)
  • 80 gms Paneer (Cut Into Small Cubes)
  • 1 pinch Salt
  • 3 Tablespoons Coriander Leaves (Chopped)

Steps:

  1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
  2. Sauté the onions in oil till they turn soft and light brown. Add the tomato and green chillies, then sauté till the tomatoes turn soft.
  3. Add the paneer, mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add salt to taste.
  4. Cook MAGGI Masala Noodles as per pack instructions and add the paneer preparation, mix well.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot.
